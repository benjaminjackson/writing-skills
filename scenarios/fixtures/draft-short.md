# Short Draft

It is important to note that the committee has been working in a very collaborative way to begin the process of improving the onboarding experience. There are a number of areas where we can make improvements, and this document will outline some of the key opportunities that we should consider as we move forward.

The first thing is communication. New members often do not know who to ask about benefits, security training, or the calendar of required meetings. This creates a situation where people feel like they are behind before they have actually started.

In order to fix this, we should create a concise first-week checklist, assign a named buddy, and remove redundant meetings from the initial schedule.
