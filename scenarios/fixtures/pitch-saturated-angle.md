# Pitch Saturated Angle

Working headline: AI data centers are using too much water

Thesis: The boom in generative AI is driving a surge in data-center water and energy demand. The story would argue that consumers do not understand the environmental cost of each prompt and that tech companies are hiding behind vague sustainability commitments.

Evidence in hand:

- Public sustainability reports from major cloud providers.
- Local news coverage of data-center water fights in drought-prone regions.
- Academic work estimating water use for AI model training and inference.

Concern: This angle may already be heavily covered. The meeting should pressure-test whether there is a newer, narrower version of the story worth pitching.
